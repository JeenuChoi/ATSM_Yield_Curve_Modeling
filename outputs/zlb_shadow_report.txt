3. Theoretical Shadow Rate Discussion (Academic Defense):
   - Problem: Gaussian ATSM allows negative rates, but observed rates are bounded at 0%.
   - Defense: Following Kim & Wright (2005) and Wu & Xia (2016), the period of ZLB
     often distorts the 'Expectations' component of the yield curve.
   - Conclusion: By comparing ZLB and Normal regimes, we find that the link between
     Inflation Uncertainty and Term Premium remains consistent, suggesting that
     our model captures the 'risk price' dynamics effectively even when short rates are stuck.
